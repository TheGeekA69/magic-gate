								Scrcpy
								
That unintelligible name stands for Screen Copy. It is the original work of Genymobile and is available here:
	https://github.com/Genymobile/scrcpy

It allows you to mirror the screen of your device and interact with it using mouse and keyboard.

Left click = tap
Rigth click = back
Middle click = home

Use CTRL/CMD - O to turn off the physical display.
Use CTRL/CMD - F to toggle fullscreen.
Use CTRL/CMD - UP/DOWN to turn volume up or down.
Use CTRL/CMD - P to turn on/off the device.
